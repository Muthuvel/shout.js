Realtime collaborative drum machine and sampling

Shout.js lets you construct a groove and collaborate on it in realtime across the web. Changes to the pattern are automatically pushed to all clients.

Call the phone number displayed and record new samples which you can then add to the pattern.

Copy the url and share it with your friends to start collaborating...
